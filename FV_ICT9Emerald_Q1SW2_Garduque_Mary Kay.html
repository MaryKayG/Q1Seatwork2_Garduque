Characters from Jose Rizals "Noli Me Tangere"

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin

Crisostomo Ibarra is the main protagonist of this book. His father is Don Rafael Ibarra. He left to study in Europe in 1874 and returned after seven years.


Noli Me Tangere Wiki

Maria Clara de los Santos

Maria Clara or Maria Clara de los Santos is Crisostomo Ibarras love interest in the book. Maria Clara is the daughter of Padre Damaso and Donya Pia, her mother had died during childbirth. She was raised by Kapitan Tiago and Tiya Isabel.


Noli Me Tangere Wiki

Padre Damaso

Padre Damaso is a priest. He is the real father of Maria Clara. He looks down on Filipinos as he thinks that they are lazy and stupid.


Noli Me Tangere Wiki

Kapitan Tiago

Kapitan Tiago was rich. He was the acting father of Maria Clara. He was the one who celebrated Ibarras return to the Philippines.


Noli Me Tangere Wiki

Donya Pia Alba

Donya Pia was Maria Claras mother, sadly she died of childbirth. She was the wife of Kapitan Tiago. She was a victim to rape.


Noli Me Tangere Wiki

Donya Victorina

Donya Victorina is what you would call a social climber. She got married to a Spaniard. She is one of Kapitan Tiago's friends.
